About LocalBite

Serving Authentic Maharashtrian Flavors Since 2025

Our Story

LocalBite was born from a simple idea: to bring the authentic taste of Maharashtra to your doorstep. Founded in Navi Mumbai, we're passionate about preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of our region.

As a cloud kitchen, we focus on what we do best - cooking delicious, authentic Maharashtrian food using traditional recipes and fresh, local ingredients. Our mission is to make these beloved dishes accessible to everyone in and around Navi Mumbai.
