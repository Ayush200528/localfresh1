Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM, Monday through Sunday. Last order accepted at 9:30 PM.

Which areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to Panvel, Nerul, Vashi, Kharghar, Kamothe, Seawoods, Sanpada, and Juinagar.

What is your delivery time?

We deliver within 45 minutes from order confirmation. During peak hours, it might take up to 60 minutes.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes! We have a wide range of vegetarian Maharashtrian dishes. All our snacks and most meals are vegetarian.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it by calling our customer support.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery, UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Digital Wallets.
